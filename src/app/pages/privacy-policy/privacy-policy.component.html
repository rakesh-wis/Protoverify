COOKIE MANAGEMENT ON THE WEBSITE:

A Cookie, or HTTP/ Internet Cookie, is a small text file that is built specifically for Internet web browsers to track, personalize, and save information about each user's session (i.e., time spent on a website) and may be used, for example, to collect information about website activity. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall Personal Information previously indicated by a user. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them. There are many ways to manage your cookies:

  1. You can refuse your consent; or
  2. You can disable PSPL or third-party cookies by use of your browser’s cookie settings; or
  3. You can use our cookie management tool to disable PSPL or third-party cookies and allow only the absolutely necessary ones.

Most internet browsers are initially set up to automatically accept cookies. If you do not want our Platform to store cookies on your device, you can change your browser settings in order to receive a notification before certain cookies are stored. You can also adjust your settings so that your browser refuses most of our cookies and only accepts certain and/ or necessary cookies. You can also withdraw your consent to cookies by deleting the cookies that have already been stored. However, disabling cookies might impact your experience while on the Platform, like restricting the use or facilities, or unique User-experience that you may avail through the same.
